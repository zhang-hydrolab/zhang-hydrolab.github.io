    Yongqiang Zhang's lab: Large Scale Hydrology

    Dr. Yongqiang Zhang is a Distinguished Professor in Hydrology and Water Resources at the Institute of Geographic Sciences and Natural Resources Research, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He also serves as the Deputy Director of the Key Laboratory of Land Water Cycle and Surface Processes. Professor Zhang is a Fellow of the Modelling and Simulation Society of Australia and New Zealand and a Fellow of the Alexander von Humboldt Foundation in Germany. He currently holds leadership roles as the Director of the Hydro-Geography Committee of the Geographical Society of China and chairs the Hydrometeorology Stream of the Modelling and Simulation Society of Australia and New Zealand. In addition, Professor Zhang serves on the editorial boards of five major international journals, including Journal of Geophysical Research: Atmospheres, Journal of Hydrology, and Remote Sensing of Environment. Over the past five years, he has led seven key research projects, including grants from the National Key Research and Development Program, a key project of the National Natural Science Foundation, and the Talent Program of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

    Professor Zhang has devoted his career to large scale hydrology, with a special focus on evapotranspiration mechanisms and their eco-hydrological effects. He has published 260 papers in peer-reviewed journals, including prestigious titles such as Science, Nature Water, and Nature Communications. In the past five years alone, he has authored or co-authored 70 papers as first or corresponding author in top-tier journals. His research has been cited more than 21,000 times, and he has an H-index of 70. His most cited first-authored paper has accumulated over 700 citations. In addition, Professor Zhang has ten ESI Highly Cited Papers and has been recognized as an Elsevier Highly Cited Chinese Researcher for four consecutive years.

    The team led by Professor Zhang comprises professors, associate professors, postdoc fellows, doctoral candidates, and master's students, totaling nearly 25 members. Students in the team are primarily recruited from leading universities such as Tsinghua University, Beijing Normal University, Northwest A&F University, and Hohai University. The team actively collaborates with top researchers both in China and abroad and has established long-term academic partnerships with internationally renowned institutions, including Ghent University, Vienna University of Technology, Pacific Northwest National Laboratory in the United States, and the Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation (CSIRO) in Australia.

    Under Professor Zhang's supervision, students and young researchers from both China and abroad have thrived in a dynamic, interdisciplinary research environment. Domestically, team members regularly present at major national conferences and have received numerous awards for young scientists. International students and visiting scholars contribute diverse perspectives, with many going on to secure positions at leading global institutions. This cultivation of next-generation talent underscores the team's lasting impact on the hydrological sciences worldwide.

    张教授致力于大尺度水文学研究，特别关注蒸散发机制及其生态水文效应。他在同行评审期刊上发表了260篇论文，包括Science、Nature Water和Nature Communications等著名期刊。仅在过去五年，他就以第一作者或通讯作者身份在顶级期刊发表了70篇论文。他的研究已被引用超过21,000次，H指数为70。他的被引次数最多的第一作者论文累计引用超过700次。此外，张教授拥有10篇ESI高被引论文，并连续四年被评为爱思唯尔中国高被引学者。

    张教授领导的团队由教授、副教授、博士后、博士研究生和硕士研究生组成，共有近25名成员。团队学生主要来自清华大学、北京师范大学、西北农林科技大学和河海大学等国内一流大学。团队积极与国内外顶尖研究人员合作，并与国际知名机构建立了长期学术合作关系，包括根特大学、维也纳工业大学、美国西北太平洋国家实验室和澳大利亚联邦科学与工业研究组织（CSIRO）。

    在张教授的指导下，来自中国和国外的学生和青年研究人员在一个充满活力的跨学科研究环境中茁壮成长。在国内，团队成员定期在主要的全国性会议上发表演讲，并获得了众多青年科学家奖项。国际学生和访问学者贡献了多元化的视角，许多人随后在全球领先机构获得了职位。这种对下一代人才的培养凸显了该团队对全球水文科学的持久影响。
